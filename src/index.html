About Me

My name is Adam Bazzi, I am a junior developer from Windsor, Ontario. I have always been enamored with technology, starting with just playing video games on my home PC. It wasn't until my cousin gave me a burned CD with Ubuntu 8.04 where I realized that computers could be used for other things then word documents and video games. Highschool provided me with my first experience programming by learning Turing, Java, and Python. Once Highschool was done I entered the Mobile Application Development programming at St. Clair College, there I was guided to where I am now today. I look forward to meeting new colleagues and potential employees.

I also really love a good cup of black coffee.
